    - geolocation (point) - PostGIS geometry
    - locality (text)

    **Tab: "Property Details"**
    - bhkType (select): 1 BHK, 2 BHK, 3 BHK, 4+ BHK, Studio, Villa, Penthouse
    - propertyType (select): Apartment, House, Villa, Condo, Farmhouse
    - furnishingStatus (select): Furnished, Semi-Furnished, Unfurnished
    - bedrooms (number, min: 0)
    - bathrooms (number, min: 0)
    - maxGuests (number, min: 1, defaultValue: 2)
    - floor (group): current (number), total (number)
    - facing (select): North, South, East, West, North-East, North-West, South-East, South-West

    **Tab: "Pricing"**
    - nightlyPrice (number, admin description: "Base price per night (INR)")
    - currency (select, defaultValue: 'INR'): INR, USD, EUR
    - seasonalPricing (array):
      - seasonName (text, required)
      - startDate (date, required)
      - endDate (date, required)
      - price (number, required)
    - minNights (number, defaultValue: 1)
    - maxNights (number)

    - houseRules (textarea)
    - petPolicy (select): Pets Allowed, Pets Not Allowed, Cats Only, Dogs Only
    - tenantPreference (select): Families, Couples, Business Travelers, Anyone

    **Tab: "Media"**
    - gallery (relationship, relationTo: 'media', hasMany: true, isSortable: true)
    - featuredImage (relationship, relationTo: 'media')
